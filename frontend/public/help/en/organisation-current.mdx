# Current Organisation

View and manage your organisation details and team members.

## What You Can Do

### View Members
See all team members in your organisation with their avatars and names. Your own membership is marked with "You".

### Invite Team Members (Administrators)
Click **Invite someone** to send email invitations to new team members.

### Edit Organisation (Administrators)
Click **Edit organisation** to modify:
- Organisation name
- Description
- Other settings

### Create New Organisation (Administrators)
Click **Create organisation** to set up a new organisation for managing additional teams or companies.

## Tips

<Tip>Keep your organisation name clear and recognizable</Tip>

<Tip>Regularly review your member list for access control</Tip>
