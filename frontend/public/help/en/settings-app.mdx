# App Settings

Customize your Lasius experience by configuring interface preferences.

## What You Can Do

### Choose Your Language
Select your preferred interface language from the dropdown

### Select Your Theme
Pick a theme that suits your preference

### Control Onboarding Tutorial
Toggle the onboarding tutorial on or off

Click **Save Settings** to apply all your changes. The page will reload to activate your new preferences.

## Tips

<Tip>Changing the language will reload the app to apply translations throughout</Tip>

<Tip>System theme automatically switches between light and dark based on your device settings</Tip>

<Tip>Turn on the onboarding tutorial if you want a refresher on how to use Lasius features</Tip>

<Tip>All settings are saved locally and will persist across sessions but not across devices</Tip>
