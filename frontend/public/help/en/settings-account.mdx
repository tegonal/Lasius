# Account Settings

Manage your personal information and account details.

## What You Can Do

### Update Profile Information
Edit your account details in the form:
- **Role** - Your role is determined by your organisation administrator (read-only)
- **First name** - Your given name
- **Last name** - Your family name
- **Email** - Your email address (can only be changed if using internal authentication)

Click **Save changes** to apply your updates.

## Tips

<Tip>Your email address can only be changed if you are using the internal authentication provider</Tip>

<Tip>Email changes may require verification</Tip>

<Tip>All changes are saved immediately after clicking Save</Tip>
