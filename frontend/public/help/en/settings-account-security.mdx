# Account Security

Update your password to keep your account secure.

## What You Can Do

### Change Password
Update your account password using the form:
1. Enter your current **Password**
2. Enter your **New password**
3. **Confirm new password** by entering it again
4. Click **Show passwords** (<Icon name="Eye" />) to reveal password fields if needed
5. Click **Save changes** to apply the new password

## Password Requirements

Your password should:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contains at least one uppercase letter
- Contains at least one number
